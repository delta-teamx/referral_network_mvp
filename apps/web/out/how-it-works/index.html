How It Works

How Referral Nova Works

From sign-up to signed deal in four simple steps. Our AI does the matchmaking so you can focus on building relationships that generate revenue.

Step 01

Create Your Profile

Tell us about your business, your ideal client, and the services you offer. Our guided wizard takes under 5 minutes and captures everything the AI needs to find your perfect referral partners.

  • Business name & category
  • Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
  • Services & specialties
  • Barter / trade preferences
Step 02

Record a 60-Second Video

A short intro video lets other members see the person behind the business. Our AI also transcribes your video to surface keywords and context that text fields alone would miss.

  • Webcam or upload
  • AI-powered transcription
  • Auto keyword extraction
  • Builds instant trust
Step 03

AI Matches You Automatically

Our 7-factor matching engine continuously scans the network, scoring every possible connection on industry fit, ICP overlap, referral potential, and more. You wake up to a curated feed every morning.

  • 7-factor scoring engine
  • Daily match digest
  • Ranked by fit quality
  • Improves over time
Step 04

Book & Close

Found a strong match? Send an intro request. Once accepted, book a Zoom call right inside Referral Nova. After the call, log the outcome so the AI can learn and refine future matches.

  • One-click intro request
  • Built-in Zoom scheduling
  • Outcome tracking
  • Smarter matches over time

Matching Engine

The AI Behind the Matches

Every potential connection is scored across seven dimensions. The result is a ranked feed of people who can actually move the needle for your business.

Industry Alignment

Pairs businesses in complementary verticals that naturally refer to each other.

ICP Matching

Compares your ideal client profile with what the other member actually serves.

Referral Compatibility

Measures the likelihood both sides can send high-quality referrals back and forth.

Keyword Overlap

Analyzes services, specialties, and profile text for semantic similarity.

Location Proximity

Factors in geography when local presence matters for the referral.

Barter Exchange

Identifies members open to trading services, creating win-win value exchanges.

Video Transcript Analysis

Extracts context, tone, and niche expertise from your recorded intro video.

After a Match

A match is just the beginning. Here is how Referral Nova guides you from introduction to outcome.

1

Intro Request
