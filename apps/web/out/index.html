How it works

From sign-up to your first deal in 4 steps

Step 01

Create your profile

Tell us your business, who you serve, who you want to meet, and who you can refer.

Step 02

Record your intro

A 60-second video so members see the person behind the business. AI transcribes it for matching.

Step 03

AI finds your matches

Our engine scans every member and surfaces the people you should meet - with a reason why.

Step 04

Book & close deals

Request an intro, book a Zoom call directly from the platform, and track every outcome.
